To delete your account, please send a request to our customer support email with the following information to verify your identity.

  1. email subject: Request to delete Gravity Jump nickname
  2. email address: rhsoh1@gmail.com
  3. Required information to include:
    • nickname: The exact nickname you use in the game

importance: Your nickname is essential information for verifying ownership. Providing inaccurate information may delay or prevent processing.

data deleted

Once your account deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.**

  • Data stored on servers:
    • Anonymous authentication account information generated through Frebase
    • User Profile (Nickname)
    • Leaderboard rankings and score records

    ※ Data stored on your device will also be deleted if you delete the app or reset the app data.

Data kept

Even after your account is deleted, de-identified statistical data processed in a form that does not identify you may be retained for a certain period of time for the purpose of improving service quality and for statistical analysis.

processing period

After receiving your account deletion request, your information will be verified and processed within 14 business days. Once processing is complete, you will receive a reply to the email address you provided.
